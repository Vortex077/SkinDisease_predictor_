STEPS TO TAKE THE PICTURE

STEP 1

Ensure well-lit conditions when capturing images. Natural light is ideal, but if not possible, use soft, diffused artificial lighting to reduce shadows and ensure clear visibility of skin features.

STEP 2

Choose a neutral background to minimize distractions and allow the AI to focus on the skin. Position the camera/smartphone so that the entire face or the specific area of interest is well-framed in the image.

STEP 3

Try to capture images in consistent poses and angles for comparison over time. Consider providing photos from the front, side, and at different angles to offer a comprehensive view for analysis.
